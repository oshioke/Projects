About Me

I am 23 years old and a junior here at NEIU. My major is computer science with the emphasis of information technology. I transferred from Northern Illinois University last fall semester. I did this because I wanted to move back to Chicago. I believe a change in my surroundings was needed for me to finish my bachelors degree. I am currently taking client side web development to learn how to create websites and also satisfy the department's requirements. Please refer to my template for an example of me using cascading style sheets on tables.

Perseverance . Upon reading, this twelve letter word seems like a long word. Unfortunately for some, the time needed to accomplish a goal will take even longer than this word. The Six XHTML rules

  • Documents must be well formed
  • All tags must nest properly and not overlap
  • Use all lower case for element names
  • Always use closing tags
  • Empty elements are marked with a closing slash
  • Attribute values must be contained in quotation marks
